How It Works

Three steps from
upload to paid.

The average freelancer loses 3–5 hours a month to file re-sends, invoice follow-ups, and "which version was final?" messages. VoxaBase eliminates all of it.

01
Create a client portal

02
Upload your deliverables

Drag in your PDFs, images, videos, or any file. They appear instantly in the portal, ready for your client to view and download — no login required on their end.

FAQ

Questions we get asked.

Do my clients need to create an account?

No. Clients click the link and immediately see their files. No signup, no password, no friction — just their deliverables.

What file types can I upload?

Any file type — PDFs, images, videos, ZIP archives, Word docs, spreadsheets, and more. If you can save it, you can deliver it.

How does payment collection work?

You add an invoice amount to your portal. When the client clicks "Pay Invoice," they pay securely via Stripe. The money goes directly to your connected bank account.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All files are encrypted in transit and at rest. Portal links are unique and unguessable. You can also password-protect any portal on Pro for sensitive projects.

Can I update files after sharing the link?

Yes. Upload a new version any time — the link stays the same. Your client always sees the latest files without you having to resend anything.

Do clients have to pay to see their files?

Files are always accessible before payment. This protects both sides — clients know exactly what they are getting, and freelancers have a clear record of delivery if payment is ever disputed.

Do I need a Stripe account?

Yes, to collect payments you will connect your Stripe account — it takes about 5 minutes. If you only need file delivery, no Stripe account is required.

Can I use my own domain for portals?

Custom domains are available on the Agency plan. Your clients will see yourcompany.com/client-name instead of voxabase.com.
