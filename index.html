How it works

Three steps from
upload to paid.

Stop sending files through email. Give every client a clean, professional portal that makes you look like a studio — not a freelancer.

01
Create a client portal

Log in, click "New Portal," and type your client's name. VoxaBase generates a clean, branded link in seconds — like voxabase.com/acme-corp.

02
Upload your deliverables

Drag in your PDFs, images, videos, or any file. They appear instantly in the portal, ready for your client to view and download — no login required on their end.

03
Send the link and get paid

Share the link. Your client sees their files and a "Pay Invoice" button. They pay via Stripe — you get notified and the money hits your account. Done.

Features

Everything in one link.
