How it works

Three steps from
upload to paid.

Most freelancers lose hours each month to file re-sends, "which version is final?" threads, and chasing invoices. VoxaBase replaces all of it with one link.

Step 01
Create a client portal

Name your client, and VoxaBase builds a clean, branded link in seconds — like voxabase.com/you/acme-corp.

Step 02
Upload your deliverables

Drag in PDFs, images, video, or any file. They appear instantly, ready for your client to view and download — no login on their end.

Step 03
Send the link, get paid

Your client sees their files and a Pay Invoice button. They pay through Stripe, and the money lands in your account.

Features

Everything in one link.

One tool instead of scattered folder links, a separate invoicing app, and endless email threads. Spend less time managing delivery and more time on the work you charge for.

File delivery

Upload any file type — PDFs, images, video, archives. Clients get a clean download button, no account needed.

Your brand, not ours

Add your logo and accent color. Clients land on a page that looks like your studio built it — because you did.

Invoice & payment

Attach an invoice to any portal. Clients pay by card through Stripe, right on the page. No more chasing.

One shareable link

Send it over email, Slack, or text. It works everywhere and never expires unless you want it to.

Revision rounds

Upload a new version to the same portal. Clients always see the latest files — you never resend a thing.

Activity tracking

See when a client opened the portal and how many times. Know where things stand without asking.
